Welcome to DVC Hospital and Research Center

We are an Intesive care hospital in Vadlamudi, Guntur District. One of the leading hospitals in the city and is known for its state-of-the-art facilities and infrastructure. We have a team of highly skilled and experienced doctors who provide the best possible treatment for our patients. We offers a wide range of services including, but not limited to, cardiology, neurology, nephrology, oncology, orthopedics, and gastroenterology.

The doctors who practice in our hospital are highly trained and experienced professionals who are dedicated to providing the best possible medical care to their patients. They are also committed to teaching and research, and many of our doctors are involved in clinical trials and other research projects.
